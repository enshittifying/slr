Introducing the Bluebook Online

Complete access to The Bluebook: A Uniform System of Citation, the go-to guide for legal citation trusted by legal professionals since 1926. Designed on an accessible, mobile-optimized platform to support quick and easy searches, the Bluebook Online is packed with personalization features to fit your needs.

New for the 22nd Edition Online

  • Searchable Index
  • Page numbers corresponding to the print book
  • Filter tools to quickly access abbreviations

A familiar experience

Experienced Bluebookers will feel at home right away. Switch seamlessly between the print and online versions, with identical numbering and content for rules and tables, all enhanced for the ideal browser experience.

Mobile optimized

On the go? The Bluebook is mobile-optimized for quick, efficient answers to your questions.

  • Designed for smaller screens
  • Single column layout
  • Simple “thumb friendly” navigation

Find what you need, when you need it

Identifying the correct citation format is easier than ever through the new intelligent search bar.

  • Tailored, instant search results.
  • Search-as-you-type performance helps eliminate errors and maximize efficiency
  • Rule previews to help you find the exact rule you need
  • Advanced search allows you to find an exact phrase by placing quotation marks around search terms
  • Limit your search to the right audience by filtering search results to the Bluepages or the Whitepages
  • Prioritized and optimized search results
  • Highlighted search terms
  • A full search results page

  • Save, organize, and share your pins easily
  • Use pins to build the citation reference experience most suited to your needs
  • Pins migrate automatically from 21st to 22nd edition
