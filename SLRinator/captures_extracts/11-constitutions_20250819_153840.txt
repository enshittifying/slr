When citing a constitution contained in an electronic database, indicate parenthetically the name of the publisher, editor, or compiler unless the constitution is published, edited, or compiled by or under the supervision of, federal or state officials. Also indicate the name of the database and information regarding the currentness of the database as provided by the database itself:

P.R. Const.
art. II, § 1 (LexisNexis, LEXIS through June 2024).
