12.10
Short Forms for Statutes
(pp. 139–40)
(a) Text.
In law review text and footnote text, use the forms listed in the “Text” column of the table below to refer to statutes. Provide a citation (in full or short form according to
rule 12.10(b)
) in an accompanying footnote when appropriate.
(b) Citations.
In law review citations, use any of the forms listed in the “Short Citation” column of the table below that clearly identifies a statute if the statute has already been cited (in either full or short form, including “
id.
”) in either the
same footnote
or in a manner such that it can be readily found in
one of the preceding five footnotes
, again including “
id.
” Otherwise, use the “Full Citation” form.
