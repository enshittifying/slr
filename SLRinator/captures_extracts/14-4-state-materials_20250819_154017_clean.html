(c) Administrative adjudications and arbitrations.

Cite according to rule 14.3 if there is an official reporter or if the decision will later be published in an official reporter. Most state administrative adjudications are not reported in an official reporter. In those cases, cite the name of the adjudication pursuant to rule 14.3.1, the number of the case, an electronic database citation where the source can be found, and include a parenthetical with the state name abbreviated pursuant to table T10, the agency name abbreviated pursuant to table T6, and the year of the decision:

Emily Burke, No. 12-0008 RI, 2012 WL 1061151 (Mo. Admin. Hearing Comm’n 2012).

Fiesta Liquors, Inc., No. 615846, 2007 WL 3315029 (Tex. Off. of Admin. Hearings 2007).

If the decision has not yet been published on an electronic database, append a link to the slip opinion following the parenthetical and omit the electronic database citation:
