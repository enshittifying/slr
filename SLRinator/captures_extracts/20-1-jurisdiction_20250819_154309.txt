20.1
Jurisdiction
(p.207)
When citing any non-U.S. source, whether in English or in another language, indicate parenthetically the jurisdiction issuing the source, abbreviated according to
table T10
:
Chase v. Campbell, [1962] S.C.R. 425 (Can.).
Berry v. Dorsey
(1975) 101 ALR 35 (Austl.).
The parenthetical is located at the end of the citation but before any parallel citations, unless otherwise indicated in
table T2
:
Canada Act, 1982, c 11 (U.K.),
reprinted in
R.S.C. 1985, app II, no 44 (Can.).
Control of Fishing for Salmon Order 2024 (SI 81/2024) (Ir.),
https://www.irishstatutebook.ie/pdf/2024/en.si.2024.0081.pdf
(on file with author).
