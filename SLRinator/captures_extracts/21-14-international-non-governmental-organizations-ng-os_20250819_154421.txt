21.14
International Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs)
(p.243)
Cite materials from international NGOs analogously to United Nations materials in
rule 21.7
. Include the name of the organization, including the appropriate abbreviation according to
rule 21.13
; the title of the document; the document number, if any; and the date:
Amnesty International,
Sierra Leone: Ending Impunity an Opportunity Not to Be Missed
, AI Index AFR 51/60/2000 (July 26, 2000).
