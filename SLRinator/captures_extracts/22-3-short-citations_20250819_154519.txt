22.3
Short Citation Form
(p.249)
Short citation forms for Tribal Nations follow the corresponding short citation forms for sources of the same type. Thus, see
rule 10.9
for Tribal cases;
rule 11
for Tribal constitutions;
rule 12.10
for Tribal codes and orders, ordinances, and resolutions; and
rule 21.17(a)
for Tribal treaties.
