23.4
Institutional Affiliation
(pp. 251–52)
When a document was prepared for a particular institution (e.g., a government agency, a company, or a nonprofit) and is not archived with that institution, add an institutional affiliation into the citation for clarity. Abbreviate according to
tables T6
,
T7
,
T10
, and
T13
. However, do not omit “
in
” or “
of
”:
Kristof Zeitman, Report on the Feasibility of a Reversible Tourbillion Movement, Jaegar LeCoultre SA 28 (1993) (on file with Am. Horological Archive, Foreign Horology Collection, Box 2, Folder 1).
This rule does not apply to Justices’ papers (
rule 23.7.1
) or other courts’ papers (
rule 23.7.2(b)
).
