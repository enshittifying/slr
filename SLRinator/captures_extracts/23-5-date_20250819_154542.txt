23.5
Date
(p.252)
Use the date printed or signed on the document. If the document is undated, or if the date is obscured or otherwise impossible to read (see
rule 23.10(c)
), indicate “
n.d
.” within the parenthetical:
Thomas Jefferson, Ice Cream Recipe (n.d.) (on file with Libr. of Cong., Thomas Jefferson Papers, Series I, Microfilm Reel 056).
If contextual information or secondary sources are able to isolate the date of authorship to an approximate year or year range, indicate that parenthetically with the prefix “
c.
” meaning “
circa.
”
Abraham Lincoln, Meditation on the Divine Will (c. Sep. 1862) (on file with Brown Univ., John Hay Library, John Hay Personal Library).
