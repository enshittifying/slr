Public domain citation format:
Montana has adopted a public domain citation format for cases after January 1, 1998. The format is:
Mont. Env’t Info. Ctr. v. Dep’t of Env’t Quality, 1999 MT 248, 296 Mont. 207, 988 P.2d 1236.
Mont. Env’t Info. Ctr. v. Dep’t of Env’t Quality, 1999 MT 248, ¶ 21, 296 Mont. 207, 988 P.2d 1236.
For additional instruction, consult Order
In re
: Opinion Forms and Citation Standards of the Supreme Court of Montana, and the Adoption of Public Domain and Neutral-Format Citation (Dec. 16, 1997), and Order in the Matter of Amending Citations Standards for the Montana Supreme Court (Jan. 22, 2009).
