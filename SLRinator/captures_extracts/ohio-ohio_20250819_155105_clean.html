Public domain citation format: Ohio has adopted a public domain citation format for cases decided after April 30, 2002. The format is:

State v. Lynch, 98 Ohio St. 3d 514, 2003-Ohio-2284, 787 N.E.2d 1185.

State v. Lynch, 98 Ohio St. 3d 514, 2003-Ohio-2284, 787 N.E.2d 1185, at ¶ 3.

For additional information, consult the Supreme Court of Ohio Writing Manual (2d ed. 2013).
