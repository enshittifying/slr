Public domain citation format:
Oklahoma has adopted a public domain citation format for cases after May 1, 1997. The format is:
Herbert v. Okla. Christian Coal., 1999 OK 90, 992 P.2d 322.
Herbert v. Okla. Christian Coal., 1999 OK 90, ¶ 2, 992 P.2d 322, 325.
For additional information, consult Oklahoma Supreme Court Rule 1.200(e) and Oklahoma Criminal Appeals Rule 3.5(c).
