Public domain citation format:
Puerto Rico has adopted a public domain citation format for cases decided after December 31, 1997. The format is:
Spanish:
Guzman Rosario v. Departamento de Hacienda, 98 TSPR 148.
English:
Guzman Rosario v. Departamento de Hacienda, 98 PRSC 148.
