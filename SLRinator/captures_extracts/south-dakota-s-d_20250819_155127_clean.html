Public domain citation format: South Dakota has adopted a public domain citation format for cases after December 31, 1995. The format is:

Wulf v. Senst, 2003 SD 105, 669 N.W.2d 135.

Wulf v. Senst, 2003 SD 105, ¶ 14, 669 N.W.2d 135, 141.

For additional information, consult South Dakota Rules of Civil Procedure § 15-26A-69.1.
