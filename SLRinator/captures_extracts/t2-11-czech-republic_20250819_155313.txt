Constitution
Czech law reflects the following hierarchical order: The Constitution, European Union legislation, constitutional laws, codes, ordinary laws, and other legal acts. There are several laws that are considered to be of constitutional importance in Czech law. Cite according to the format for statutes, appending the description of the law if desired:
Ústavní zákon č. 1/1993 Sb., Ústava České Republiky [Constitution of Czechia].
