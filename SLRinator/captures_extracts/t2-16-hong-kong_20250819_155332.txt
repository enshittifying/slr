Hong Kong
(Common Law)
Chinese and English language sources
Hong Kong has two official languages: English and Chinese. Where a source is available in both languages, generally cite to the English source, unless there is a specific reason to cite to the Chinese version. When citing an English language primary source, it is not necessary to preface the source with the words “
translated in
.
