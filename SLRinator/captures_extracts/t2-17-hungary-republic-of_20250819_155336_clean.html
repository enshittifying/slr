Statutes and decrees

Where available, cite statutes to Magyar Közlöny (MK) (Official Gazette of Hungary) and decrees to the specific ministerial gazette, e.g., Honvédelmi Közlöny (HK) (Gazette of National Defense).

Citation format for törvény(ek) (act(s)): <year of issuance>. <number of the act within that year>. <scope of regulation> (<English translation of the name of the act>) (<country abbreviation if not evident from context>).

2012. évi XXXVI. törvény az Országgyűlésről(Act XXXVI of 2012 on the Parliament) (Hung.).

2013. évi V. törvény a Polgári Törvénykönyvről (Act V of 2013 on the Civil Code) (Hung.).

Citation format for decrees: <decree number>/<year of issuance>. (<month of issuance, in roman numerals>.<day>.) <abbreviation of issuer> <abbreviation of type of decree> <scope of regulation> (<English translation of the name of the decree>) (<country abbreviation if not evident from context>).

When following the format above, use “Korm.” (Government), “FVM” (Minister of Agriculture and Rural Development), “EüM” (Minister of Health), and so on. For governmental and ministerial decrees, use “r.” for “rendelet” (decree); for municipal decrees, use “ör.” for “önkormányzati rendelet.”
