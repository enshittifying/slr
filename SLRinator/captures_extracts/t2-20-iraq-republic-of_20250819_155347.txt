Courts
Mah
.
kamat al-Tamyīz
(Tamyīz) (Court of Cassation: highest court of ordinary jurisdiction, sits only in the capital and has several chambers). Citation is sufficiently recognized by referring to this court as “Mah. kamat al-Tamyīz” or simply “Tamyīz.”
Tamyīz [Cassation, civil chamber], decision No. 1509 of March 18, 1974, al-Nashrah al-Qadā’īyah, vol. 1, year 5, p. 11 (Iraq).
Mah. kamat al-Isti’nāf
(Isti’nāf) (Court of Appeal: general court of second instance). Cite with the name followed by the geographic area.
Isti’nāf Baghdād [Court of Appeal, Baghdad], decision No. 48 of September 1985, Majmū‘at al-Ahkām al-‘Adlīyah [Compilation of Judicial Decisions], issues 3 and 4 of 1985, p. 141 (Iraq).

Official Gazette
The official gazette of Iraq is called
al-Waqā’i‘ al-‘Irāqīyah
and is published periodically by the Iraqi government. Cite by the issue number and date of
publication.
Citation format:
