Internet sources
The Austrian Bundeskanzleramt (Federal Chancellery) maintains Rechtsinformationssystem (BKA/RIS), a free electronic legal database available at
http://www.ris.bka.gv.at
. BKA/RIS contains the federal statutory gazette back to 1848 and links to earlier collections of laws as provided by the Austrian National Library. BKA/RIS also provides a consolidated version of federal laws as currently in effect, texts of the decisions of the courts, legislation of the Länder (states), some translations of federal laws, and some administrative directives and draft legislation.
The decisions of the Landesverwaltungsgerichte are available at Bundeskanzleramt Rechtsinformationssystem (BKA/RIS),
https://www.ris.bka.gv.at/Lvwg
; the decisions of the Bundesverwaltungsgericht are available at
https://www.ris.bka.gv.at/Bvwg
; and the decisions of the Bundesfinanzgericht are available at
https://findok.bmf.gv.at/findok?execution=e1s1&BFG_Suche=1
, a website of the Federal Ministry of Finance.
