Constitution
Constituição da República Portuguesa
[
Constitution
]
C.R.P.
When citing the Constitution for the first time, spell out the name of the Constitution in full and provide the link to an up-to-date vernacular text or translation, as provided by the website of the official gazette (
Diário da República
).
Constituição da República Portuguesa
[C.R.P.], English translation available at https://dre.pt/constitution-of-the-portuguese-republic.
The Constitution is divided into parts and titles, and subdivided into articles, numbers, and paragraphs. Do not mention a part or a title unless referring to it as a whole. When referring to a particular division or subdivision abbreviate as follows.
C.R.P., art. 6, no. 1.
C.R.P., art. 9, para. b).
C.R.P., Part 1, Title II.
Cite the Constitution currently in force without a date. If the cited provision has been amended or repealed, either indicate parenthetically the fact and year of amendment or repeal or cite the amending or repealing Constitutional Act (
Lei Constitutional
) in full.
C.R.P., art. 10 (repealed 1982).
C.R.P., art. 140, amended by Lei Constitutional n. ̊ 1/82 de 30 de setembro [Constitutional Act no. 1/82 of September 30th].
