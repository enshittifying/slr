T2.38 South Africa

(Hybrid Common Law)

Cases

Citation format: <case name, in italics> <year of publication> (<volume number>) <reporter abbreviation> <first page> (<court abbreviation>) at <page(s) of specific material, if desired and available> <paragraph, if desired> (<country abbreviation if not evident from context>).

Fraser v. Naude 1999 (1) SA 1 (CC) at 78 (S. Afr.).

When citing to a case in which the court numbers the paragraphs — a style that courts are increasingly adopting — cite to the paragraph number.

Fraser v. Naude 1999 (1) SA 1 (CC) at 78 para. 10 (S. Afr.).

Constitution

Unlike citations to statutes, citations to the Constitution and its amending laws do not include act numbers. Titles of laws amending the Constitution reflect their chronological order.

Constitution of the Republic of South Africa Act, 1996:

S. Afr. Const., 1996.

Constitution of the Republic of South Africa First Amendment Act of 1997:

S. Afr. Const., First Amendment Act of 1997.

Constitution of the Republic of South Africa Act 200 of 1993 (Interim Constitution):

S. Afr. (Interim) Const., 1993.

Statutes

Citation format: <act name> <act number> of <year of passage> <section(s), if applicable> (<country abbreviation if not evident from context>).

Interpretation Act 33 of 1957 § 6 (S. Afr.).

When citing to a statutory compilation, include the volume and name of the publication as well as the year of any updates, as shown below:

Children's Act 38 of 2005 § 1, 5 BSRSA (updated through 2012).

Other legislation

Provincial ordinances (collected by province by year) (1910–1994):

Road Traffic Ordinance 21 of 1966 (Cape Province) (S. Afr.).

Laws of provincial legislatures (1994–date):

Western Cape Colleges Law 12 of 1994 (S. Afr.).

Proclamations, regulations, and other government notices are collected in the Government Gazette (GG). When citing to documents in the GG, include: Government Notice (GN) number, GG number, and day (day month year).

Debt Counselling Regulations 2012, GN R.362 of GG 35327 (10 May 2012).

Consumer Product Safety Recall Guidelines, GN 290 of GG 35434 (13 June 2012).
