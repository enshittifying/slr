Public domain citation format:
Utah has adopted a public domain citation format for cases after December 31, 1998. The format is:
Wickham v. Galetka, 2002 UT 72, 61 P.3d 979.
Gilley v. Blackstock, 2002 UT App 414, ¶ 10, 61 P.3d 305.
For additional information, consult Utah Supreme Court Standing Order No. 4 (effective Jan. 18, 2000).
