Public domain citation format: Wisconsin has adopted a public domain citation format for cases decided after December 31, 1999. The format is:

Glaeske v. Shaw, 2003 WI App 71, 261 Wis. 2d 549, 661 N.W.2d 72.

Glaeske v. Shaw, 2003 WI App 71, ¶ 9, 261 Wis. 2d 549, 661 N.W.2d 72.

For additional information, consult Wisconsin Supreme Court Rule 80.
