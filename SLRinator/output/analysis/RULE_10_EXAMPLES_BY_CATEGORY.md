# Bluebook Rule 10 - Error Examples by Category

## 1. Case Names (19 Error Types)

### 10.1.1 - Missing Case Name in Citation
**Error**: Citation begins with reporter number without case name
- ❌ Incorrect: `410 U.S. 113 (1973)`
- ✓ Correct: `Roe v. Wade, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`

### 10.2.1 - First Names/Initials Included
**Error**: Case name includes first names or initials
- ❌ Incorrect: `John Doe v. Jane Smith, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`
- ✓ Correct: `Doe v. Smith, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`

### 10.2.2 - Middle Names/Initials Included
**Error**: Middle names or initials should be omitted
- ❌ Incorrect: `John Michael Doe v. Jane Sarah Smith, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`
- ✓ Correct: `Doe v. Smith, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`

### 10.2.3 - "The" at Beginning of Case Name
**Error**: Case name starts with "The" which should be omitted
- ❌ Incorrect: `The United States v. Nixon, 418 U.S. 683 (1974)`
- ✓ Correct: `United States v. Nixon, 418 U.S. 683 (1974)`

### 10.2.4 - Multiple Parties on Same Side
**Error**: Multiple parties on same side should be abbreviated to first party only
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith and Jones v. Brown, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Brown, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`

### 10.2.5 - Alternative Case Names (AKA, d/b/a)
**Error**: Alternative names should be omitted
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones AKA Brown, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`

### 10.2.6 - Geographic Unit Not Abbreviated
**Error**: State names in party names should be abbreviated per Table T10
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. State of California, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Cal., 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`

### 10.2.7 - Entity Name Abbreviation Errors
**Error**: Entity names should be abbreviated per Table T6
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones Company, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones Co., 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`

### 10.2.11 - Business Entity Truncation
**Error**: Entity names should be complete, not abbreviated mid-word
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith Indus. Inc.`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith Industries Inc.`

### 10.2.12 - Incorrect Use of et al.
**Error**: Et al. should not appear in case name
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones et al.`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones`

## 2. Reporter Citations (16 Error Types)

### 10.3.1 - Reporter Abbreviation Incorrect
**Error**: Reporter abbreviation should follow official designations
- ❌ Incorrect: `410 US 113 (should be U.S.)`
- ✓ Correct: `410 U.S. 113`

### 10.3.2 - Missing Second Series Designation
**Error**: Second/third series should be indicated
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E. 113`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113`

### 10.3.3 - Missing First Page Number
**Error**: Reporter abbreviation should be followed by page numbers
- ❌ Incorrect: `Roe v. Wade, 410 U.S. (1973)`
- ✓ Correct: `Roe v. Wade, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`

### 10.3.3.1 - Federal Reporter Abbreviation
**Error**: Federal reporters should use F., F.2d, F.3d not Fed.
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 Fed. 113`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 F.3d 113`

### 10.3.3.2 - Federal Appendix Citation Format
**Error**: Federal Appendix should be cited as F. App'x not F.A.
- ❌ Incorrect: `400 F.A. 113`
- ✓ Correct: `400 F. App'x 113`

### 10.3.3.3 - Supreme Court Reporter Abbreviation
**Error**: Supreme Court Reporter should be abbreviated S. Ct.
- ❌ Incorrect: `410 Supreme Court Reporter 113`
- ✓ Correct: `410 S. Ct. 113`

### 10.3.1.1 - Missing Parallel Citation When Required
**Error**: Some cases require parallel citations to all major reporters
- ❌ Incorrect: `Roe v. Wade, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`
- ✓ Correct: `Roe v. Wade, 410 U.S. 113, 93 S. Ct. 705 (1973)`

### 10.3.1.2 - Parallel Citations in Wrong Order
**Error**: Official reporter should come first
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 410 U.S. 113, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1973)`

## 3. Court and Jurisdiction (13 Error Types)

### 10.4.1 - Missing Court Designation
**Error**: Non-obvious reporters need court abbreviation
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (Ohio 1980)`

### 10.4.2 - Unnecessary Court Designation
**Error**: Obvious reporters do not need court designation
- ❌ Incorrect: `Roe v. Wade, 410 U.S. 113 (U.S. 1973)`
- ✓ Correct: `Roe v. Wade, 410 U.S. 113 (1973)`

### 10.4.3 - Court Abbreviation Not Abbreviated
**Error**: Court names should be abbreviated per Table T1
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (California 1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (Cal. 1980)`

### 10.4.4 - Federal Circuit Designation Error
**Error**: Federal Circuit should use (Fed. Cir.) not (Fed.)
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 F.3d 113 (Fed. 1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 F.3d 113 (Fed. Cir. 1980)`

### 10.4.5 - Circuit Court Designation Incomplete
**Error**: Circuit courts should be designated as (1st Cir., 2d Cir., etc.)
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 F.3d 113 (2 1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 F.3d 113 (2d Cir. 1980)`

### 10.4.6 - Wrong State Abbreviation
**Error**: Use Table T1 for state abbreviations
- ❌ Incorrect: `(Calif. 1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `(Cal. 1980)`

### 10.4.7 - Missing Periods in Abbreviation
**Error**: Abbreviations need proper periods
- ❌ Incorrect: `(D C. 1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `(D.C. 1980)`

### 10.4.8 - Generic Court Designation
**Error**: Use specific court, not generic terms
- ❌ Incorrect: `(Trial Court 1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `(N.D. Ill. 1980)`

## 4. Dates and Years (7 Error Types)

### 10.5.1 - Missing Year in Parentheses
**Error**: Citation must include year in parentheses
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980)`

### 10.5.2 - Year Not in Parentheses
**Error**: Year should be in parentheses following page number
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 1980`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980)`

### 10.5.3 - Full Date Used Instead of Year Only
**Error**: Citations should use year only, not full date
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (Jan. 15, 1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980)`

### 10.5.4 - Year Format Incorrect (Two Digits)
**Error**: Year should be full four-digit format
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (80)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980)`

### 10.5.6 - Two-Digit Year
**Error**: Years must be four digits
- ❌ Incorrect: `('80)`
- ✓ Correct: `(1980)`

### 10.5.7 - Duplicate Year Parentheses
**Error**: Only one year parenthetical needed
- ❌ Incorrect: `(1980) (1981)`
- ✓ Correct: `(1980)`

## 5. Parenthetical Information (12 Error Types)

### 10.6.1 - Missing Parenthetical with Case Holding
**Error**: Case citation should include brief holding in parenthetical
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980).`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980) (holding that...).`

### 10.6.2 - Holding Parenthetical Not Lowercase
**Error**: Parenthetical information should start with lowercase
- ❌ Incorrect: `(Holding that plaintiff was negligent)`
- ✓ Correct: `(holding that plaintiff was negligent)`

### 10.6.3 - Parenthetical Uses Wrong Tense
**Error**: Parentheticals should use past tense
- ❌ Incorrect: `(holding that court will find)`
- ✓ Correct: `(holding that court found)`

### 10.6.1.1 - Missing Overruled Parenthetical
**Error**: Overruled or reversed cases should include this in parenthetical
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980), reversed by`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980) (overruled by...)`

### 10.6.2.1 - In-Chambers Opinion Not Indicated
**Error**: In-chambers opinions should be indicated in parenthetical
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980) (per curiam)`

## 6. Prior and Subsequent History (11 Error Types)

### 10.7.1 - Missing Subsequent History
**Error**: Cases that were reversed should include this in citation
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980), rev'd, 410 U.S. 113 (1981)`

### 10.7.2 - Subsequent History Not Properly Introduced
**Error**: Subsequent history should use proper connector words
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980) reversed`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980), rev'd`

### 10.7.1.1 - Incorrect Abbreviation for Case History Term
**Error**: Use proper abbreviations (aff'd, rev'd, vacated, remanded)
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980), aff'd by...`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980), aff'd...`

### 10.7.1.2 - Using Past Tense Instead of Abbreviated Form
**Error**: Use abbreviated forms in citations
- ❌ Incorrect: `reversed by Smith v. Jones`
- ✓ Correct: `rev'd by Smith v. Jones`

### 10.7.2.1 - Different Appellate Case Name Not Indicated
**Error**: If case name differs on appeal, show both names
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980), rev'd by 410 U.S. 113 (1981)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980), rev'd, Jones v. Smith, 410 U.S. 113 (1981)`

## 7. Special Citation Forms (12 Error Types)

### 10.8.1.1 - Pending Case Cited with Publication Year
**Error**: Pending cases should not include publication year
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (pending 1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, No. 20-0001 (pending)`

### 10.8.1.2 - Unreported Case Without Docket Number
**Error**: Unreported cases should include docket number
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones (unreported)`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, No. 20-0001 (unreported)`

### 10.8.3.1 - Brief Missing Document Type Designation
**Error**: Briefs should specify type
- ❌ Incorrect: `Brief, Smith v. Jones, 400 U.S. 113 (1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `Brief for Appellant, Smith v. Jones, No. 20-0001 (filed...)`

### 10.8.3.2 - Transcript Citation Lacks Page Number
**Error**: Court transcript should include page or record reference
- ❌ Incorrect: `Transcript, Smith v. Jones, 400 U.S. 113 (1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `Transcript at 25, Smith v. Jones, 400 U.S. 113 (1980)`

### 10.8.4.1 - Administrative Order Without Court Designation
**Error**: Administrative orders must specify issuing court
- ❌ Incorrect: `Administrative Order (1980)`
- ✓ Correct: `Administrative Order No. 001, U.S. Dist. Ct. N.D. Cal. (1980)`

## 8. Short Forms for Cases (10 Error Types)

### 10.9.1 - Short Form Used Without Prior Full Citation
**Error**: Short forms can only be used after full citation
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith, 400 N.E.2d 113` (as first reference)
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1973)` [first] → `Smith, 400 N.E.2d at 115` [subsequent]

### 10.9.2 - Id. Used Incorrectly
**Error**: Id. can only be used for immediately preceding source
- ❌ Incorrect: `[Previous cite: Smith v. Jones] Jones v. Brown... Id., 400 N.E.2d`
- ✓ Correct: `[Previous cite: Smith v. Jones] Id., 400 N.E.2d at 115`

### 10.9.3 - Supra Without Clear Prior Citation
**Error**: Supra should reference specific note or location
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, supra`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, supra note 5`

### 10.9.4 - Hereinafter Abbreviation Not Properly Introduced
**Error**: Hereinafter should be in brackets after full citation
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 hereinafter SJ`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 [hereinafter SJ]`

### 10.9.5 - Short Form Includes Unnecessary Information
**Error**: Short forms should omit reporter citations and year
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980)` [as short form]
- ✓ Correct: `Smith, 400 N.E.2d at 115`

### 10.9.6 - Pin-Cite Missing from Short Form
**Error**: Short forms should include pin-cite page number
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith, 400 N.E.2d`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith, 400 N.E.2d at 115`

### 10.9.8 - Supra Note Reference to Non-Existent Note
**Error**: Supra note must reference actual footnote number
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones, supra note 50` (when only 20 notes)
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones, supra note 5`

### 10.9.9 - Hereinafter Abbreviation Not Used Consistently
**Error**: Hereinafter abbreviation must be used in subsequent references
- ❌ Incorrect: `Smith v. Jones [hereinafter SJ]... Smith v. Jones`
- ✓ Correct: `Smith v. Jones [hereinafter SJ]... SJ`

### 10.9.10 - Short Form as First Reference
**Error**: Cannot use short form as first reference
- ❌ Incorrect: `[First reference] Smith, 400 N.E.2d at 115`
- ✓ Correct: `[First reference] Smith v. Jones, 400 N.E.2d 113 (1980)`
