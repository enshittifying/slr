22.9: Dates and Date Ranges

Retain all digits when giving a range of years (for example, “1993-1998,” not “1993-98”).

When referring to decades, do not insert an apostrophe (for example, “1780s,” not “1780’s”). See RB 22.2.

References to centuries should be spelled out in lowercase (for example, “twentieth century,” not “20th century”).
