Redbook Section 2.3: Italics and Punctuation

2.3: Italics and Punctuation

Refer to The Bluebook and CMOS with regard to italics and punctuation.

One point in particular: The initial "the" in a periodical title is lowercased and not italicized (for example, "I read the New York Times," not "I read The New York Times"). See CMOS 8.170. For books in a similar context, "the" is capitalized and italicized if included (for example, "I adore The Firm," not "I adore the Firm"). See CMOS 8.169.
