4.2: Id. with "at" for Pincites

Id. should only be followed by "at" if "at" is immediately followed by a page number (including roman numerals). See BB 3.2(a), 4.1. If id. is followed by text or symbols of any kind, do not use "at." Examples:

Id. at 1235; id. at xxii.
Id. ch. 7; id. § 6.15; id. ¶¶ 141-44.
Id. amend. XVIII, § 1.
