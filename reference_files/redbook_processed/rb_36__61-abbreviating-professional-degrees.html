Redbook Section 6.1: Abbreviating Professional Degrees

6.1: Abbreviating Professional Degrees

Contrary to CMOS 10.21, SLR does not omit periods in educational degrees. Use "J.D." instead of "JD" and "Ph.D." over "PhD" (and so on). Note that we would never include an author's degree—periods or not—in a citation. See BB 15.1.
