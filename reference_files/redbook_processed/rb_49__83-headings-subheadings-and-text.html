8.3: Headings, Subheadings, and Text

SLR article headings and subheadings should be capitalized as titles per BB 8(a).

Sub-subheadings (introduced by an Arabic numeral as opposed to a letter or Roman numeral) are treated as normal text, with only the first word automatically capitalized. Textual capitalization should follow the rules laid out by BB 8(c) (primarily helpful for legal terms) and CMOS ch. 8.
