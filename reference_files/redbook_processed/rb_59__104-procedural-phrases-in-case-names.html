10.4: Procedural Phrases in Case Names

SLR always includes "ex rel." when the reporter links the named party to another party with a phrase starting with "as" or "on behalf of" or something similar. See BB 10.2.1(b). Beware that the case caption at the top of the reporter page often omits "ex rel." even in cases where SLR would include it. When in doubt, include the procedural phrase and leave a comment for your SE.
