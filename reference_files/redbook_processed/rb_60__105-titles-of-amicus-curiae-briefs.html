10.5: Titles of Amicus Curiae Briefs

Even if italicized in the original filing, do not italicize the words "amicus," "amicus curiae," or "amici curiae" in the title of an amicus brief. See RB 10.6; BB 10.8.3.

SLR prefers the use of et al. for the signatories of amicus briefs when there are three or more parties named in the brief's title. See RB 15.1.
