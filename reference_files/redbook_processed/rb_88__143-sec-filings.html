14.3: SEC Filings

Pincites for SEC filings follow the parenthetical identifying the form type and are set off by a comma. Cite section, paragraph, or item numbers where available. Use “at” only for page numbers. Example:

Alphabet Inc., Annual Report (Form 10-K), item 8, at 70 (Feb. 2, 2017).

Expanding on BB Table T1.2, SLR has created the following citation format for exhibits included in SEC filings:

[Company name (abbreviated according to BB Tables T6 & T10)], [full document name as indicated on the exhibit or table of exhibits] (Exhibit [number] to [form type]), [pincite] ([full date of filing]).

Examples:

Bear Stearns Cos., Agreement and Plan of Merger, Dated March 16, 2008, by and Between the Bear Stearns Companies Inc. and JPMorgan Chase & Co. (Exhibit 2.1 to Form 8-K), § 1.4(c) (Mar. 20, 2008).
Wal-Mart Stores, Inc., Amended and Restated Bylaws of Wal-Mart Stores, Inc., Effective as of June 5, 2014 (Exhibit 3.2 to Form 10-Q), art. II, § 9 (June 6, 2014).
