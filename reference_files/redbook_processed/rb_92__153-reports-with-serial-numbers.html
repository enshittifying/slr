Redbook Section 15.3: Reports with Serial Numbers

15.3: Reports with Serial Numbers

Extrapolating from BB 15.7, always include a report number for a government document or other report that has a serial number.

For legislative, administrative, and executive reports, see BB 13.4, 14.2(d). Follow the citation format in BB 13.4(d) for Government Accountability Office (GAO) and Congressional Research Service (CRS) reports.
